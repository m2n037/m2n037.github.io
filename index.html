Welcome to My Homepage

I am an aspiring researcher in mechanical design, finite element analysis, and flexible electronics. I intend to bring the novelty of biological entities using mechanical design methods, mechanical characterization, control integration, and machine learning.

I am working at Advanced Technology Lab, Samsung R&D Institute Bangalore as a Mechanical Engineer III from December, 2017. Before Samsung, I worked at General Motors Technical Centre India as a Senior Engineer - Safety CAE (Pedestrian Protection, Crash & Safety) from August, 2013 to Novemeber, 2017. I completed MDes (Product Design and Engineering) from Centre for Product Design and Manufacturing, Indian Institute of Science Bangalore and BTech (Mechanical Engineering) from National Institute of Technology Silchar.

I enjoy solving engineering problem, designing products, and reading books. As of now, I am working on various research and design projects with multiple collaborators from academia.

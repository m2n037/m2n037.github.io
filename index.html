Welcome to My Homepage

I am an aspiring researcher in mechanical design and reliability of wearable electronics & soft robotics. I intend to bring the novelty of biological entities using mechanical design methods, mechanical characterization,control integration, and machine learning/AI.

I am working at Advanced Technology Lab, Samsung R&D Institute Bangalore as a Technical Lead (Mechanical Design Analyst) from December, 2017. Before Samsung, I worked at General Motors Technical Centre India as a Senior Engineer - Safety CAE (Pedestrian Protection, Crash & Safety) from August, 2013 to Novemeber, 2017. I completed MDes (Product Design and Engineering) from Centre for Product Design and Manufacturing, Indian Institute of Science Bangalore and BTech (Mechanical Engineering) from National Institute of Technology Silchar.

I enjoy solving engineering problems, designing products, practicing mathematics, and writing code. As of now, I am working on various research and design projects with my advisors from IISc.

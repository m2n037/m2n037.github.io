Welcome to My Homepage

I am an aspiring researcher in computational engineering focused in finite element method applied to soft materials and flexible electronics.

I am working at Advanced Technology Lab, Samsung R&D Institute Bangalore as a Chief Engineer - Mechanical Simulation from December, 2017. Before Samsung, I worked at General Motors Technical Centre India as a Senior Engineer - Safety CAE (Pedestrian Protection, Safety and Crashworthiness) from August, 2013 to Novemeber, 2017. I completed MDes (Product Design and Engineering) from Centre for Product Design and Manufacturing, Indian Institute of Science Bangalore and BTech (Mechanical Engineering) from National Institute of Technology Silchar.

I enjoy solving engineering problems at work, exploring product design, and reading books.

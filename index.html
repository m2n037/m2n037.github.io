Welcome to My Homepage

I am an aspiring graduate student in Robotics, especially locomotion, manipulation, and grasping. I intend to bring the dexterity of animals to robots by using mechanism design, control systems, and robot learning.

I am working at General Motors Technical Centre India as a Crash and Safety CAE (Pedestrian Protection) Engineer, since August, 2013. I completed MDes (Product Design and Engineering) from Centre for Product Design and Manufacturing, Indian Institute of Science Bangalore and BTech (Mechanical Engineering) from National Institute of Technology Silchar.

I enjoy solving engineering problems, designing products, practicing mathematics, and writing code. As of now, I am working on various research and design projects with my advisors from IISc.

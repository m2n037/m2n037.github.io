Welcome to My Homepage

I am an aspiring researcher in using simulation and predictive methods to solve design and engineering problems.

I am working at Predictive Engineering & Design group, part of the Global Application Technology, SABIC Research & Technology Bangalore as Senior Scientist. Earlier I worked at the Advanced Research Group, Samsung R&D Institute Bangalore as a Chief Engineer - Mechanical Simulation. Before Samsung, I worked at General Motors Technical Centre India as a Senior Engineer - Safety CAE (Pedestrian Protection, Safety and Crashworthiness).

I completed MDes (Product Design and Engineering) from Centre for Product Design and Manufacturing, Indian Institute of Science Bangalore in 2013 and BTech (Mechanical Engineering) from National Institute of Technology Silchar in 2010.

I enjoy solving engineering problems, exploring product design, and reading books.
